"Kailash-Manasarovar Sacred Walks"

A JOURNEY OF A LIFETIME 2021

Since ancient times, streams of pilgrims have made their way to Kailash, leaving the comforts of their home behind and walking with a fire in their heart. Kailash Manasarovar is one of the most significant pilgrimage and Sadhguru says, if you are a pilgrim, you are using this outward journey to make an inward journey.

2021.11.11(WED) ~ 23(MON)
@Kathmandu, Nepal (MAP)

Main Program

Preparation

We give you the instrunctions to prepare your body and your mind to this journey.

Parikrama Mount Kailash

Trek or mountaineering the Mount Kailash. There is an option of hiring a yak or a pony.

Holy Dip

Take a dip in the sacred Manasarovar Lake, purify your soul, and clean your mind.

Closing Session

Farewell to the program. The final step, prepare yourself to return to normal life.

He has spoken at the United Nation's Millennium World Peace Summit, the British parliament's House of Lords, the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, the International Institute for Management Development, and at the annual World Economic Forum in 2007, 2017 and 2020.
